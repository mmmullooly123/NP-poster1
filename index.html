Exploring the Design Space of Peptide-Mediated Reversible Nanoparticle Aggregation

Margaret Mullooly · 2025

Motivation

Gold nanoparticles (AuNPs) are widely used in diagnostics and therapeutics due to their unique optical and chemical properties. However, their tendency to aggregate in solution can compromise performance. This project explores how peptide sequence and terminal ends affects reversible aggregation of citrate-capped AuNPs using molecular simulations.
